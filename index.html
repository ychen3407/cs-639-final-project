Our Data Source
With the rapid development of hardware and software, new games are emerging at an extremely fast pace. Also, the gaming platforms are iterating with a shorter time period than ever. With curiosity about the development of the most popular gaming hardware and the top games in recent years, we propose to answer the two following questions by the three interactive visualizations.

Question 1: What are the best games on the most popular platforms from the year 2005 to 2020?

Question 2: How well the most popular platforms are performing over the years from 2010 to 2021? The scatter plot implementation helps to answer the first question, and the bar chart and line chart implementation help to answer the second question.
